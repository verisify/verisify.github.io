About us        

Rate your Governor Platform

Our website provides a platform for Nigerian citizens to voice their opinions on the performance of their state governors across key metrics. We believe that transparent and accountable governance is essential for the progress and development of Nigeria, and our weekly poll aims to foster a healthy competition among the 37 state governors.

Each week, registered users can log in to our secure platform and rate their state governor's performance in four key areas: education, security, infrastructure, and job creation. The governor with the highest overall score at the end of the week is declared the winner.

It is important to note that we are not affiliated with the Independent National Electoral Commission (INEC) and our poll results do not have any official bearing on the electoral process.

By providing a transparent and data-driven platform for evaluating state-level governance, we aim to empower Nigerian citizens to hold their elected leaders accountable and encourage governors to prioritize the issues that matter most to their constituents. We believe that this type of civic engagement can contribute to the strengthening of Nigeria's democratic institutions and the improvement of service delivery across the country.

We welcome all Nigerian citizens to participate in our weekly poll and share their perspectives on the performance of their state governors. Together, we can work towards a more transparent, responsive, and effective system of governance in Nigeria.
